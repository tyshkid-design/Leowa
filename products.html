SYSCOOP Agri ERP

SYSCOOP Agri & Coffee

A specialist Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system for coffee, dairy, and cereal cooperatives. SYSCOOP Agri automates the entire value chain — from farm collection and weighing to quality grading, payment computation, and M-Pesa disbursement.

Real impact: One cooperative using SYSCOOP Agri reduced payment complaints by 70% and cut payment processing time to just 2 days — down from over 2 weeks with manual processes.
