Our Story

Rooted in Africa, Built for Africa

Leowa Associates Limited was established to bridge the gap between international consulting standards and deep local contextual knowledge in East Africa. We are proudly African-led, with our Managing Director Josephine Ng'ethe — a former Deloitte East Africa manager — steering a team of highly experienced professionals.

We combine international-grade methodology with genuine understanding of the Kenyan, Ugandan, and Tanzanian business landscape. This unique positioning allows us to deliver solutions that are not only technically sound but also practically implementable on the ground.

Our work spans management consulting, institutional capacity building, ICT software development, and school management systems — serving NGOs, cooperatives, government agencies, private businesses, and international development partners.
